About Roz Ka Hisab

Roz Ka Hisab is a simple family expense tracker that helps you record and manage daily expenses. It allows you to categorize spending like Food, Travel, Shopping and more, so you can clearly see where your money goes each month.

The goal is to make managing expenses easy for families by providing quick summaries, monthly totals, and a clear dashboard to stay on top of finances.

Key Features:

  • Track daily expenses in categories
  • View monthly summaries at a glance
  • Simple, clean and easy to use
  • Helps families stay organized with money
